# Privacy Policy for winlivelyrics

**Last Updated: November 2025**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how winlivelyrics ("the App", "we", "our") handles data when you use our Windows application. winlivelyrics is a desktop application that provides real-time synchronized lyrics for music playback with translation features.

## Developer Information

- **Developer:** tataouinea
- **App Name:** winlivelyrics - Live Sync Lyrics
- **Contact:** https://github.com/tataouinea

## Data Collection and Usage

### Information We Collect

**winlivelyrics does not directly collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our own servers.** We do not operate any backend infrastructure that logs or retains user data.

However, to provide lyrics and translation functionality, the App automatically shares certain information with third-party services as described below.

### Data Shared with Third-Party Services

#### 1. LRCLIB.net (Lyrics Service)

**Purpose:** To retrieve synchronized lyrics for currently playing media.

**Data Shared:**
- Song title
- Artist name
- Album name
- Track duration (in seconds)
- Your IP address (automatically included in HTTP requests)

**When:** This data is sent automatically whenever media is playing and the App is running.

**Third-Party Privacy:** LRCLIB.net may collect, log, and process this information according to their own terms and privacy policies. We do not control how LRCLIB.net handles this data.

#### 2. Google Translate

**Purpose:** To provide real-time translation of lyrics into your selected language.

**Data Shared:**
- Lyrics text
- Target language selection
- Your IP address (automatically included in HTTP requests)

**When:** This data is sent automatically when lyrics are retrieved and displayed. Translation is a core feature of the App and operates continuously while the App is running.

**Third-Party Privacy:** Google Translate is subject to Google's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy:
- [Google Terms of Service](https://policies.google.com/terms)
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

We do not control how Google collects, uses, or retains the data sent to their translation service.

## Local Data Storage

The App stores the following settings locally on your device using Windows ApplicationData:

- Translation language preference
- Sync button state (active/inactive)
- Disclaimer acceptance status

**This data never leaves your device** and is used solely to maintain your preferences across App sessions.

## Media Access

The App accesses information about currently playing media on your Windows device (e.g., from music players, browsers, media apps) to retrieve song metadata. This information is:
- Processed locally on your device
- Used only to query LRCLIB.net for lyrics
- Not stored permanently by the App

## No Personal Identification

We do not collect or process any personally identifiable information such as:
- Name or email address
- Account credentials
- Payment information
- Location data (beyond IP address visible to third-party services)
- Usage analytics or telemetry

## Children's Privacy

winlivelyrics does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The App is not directed at children, and we recommend parental supervision if used by minors.

## Data Security

Since the App does not store personal data on any servers we control, there is no centralized database that could be compromised. Settings stored locally on your device are protected by Windows' built-in security mechanisms.

However, data transmitted to LRCLIB.net and Google Translate is subject to the security practices of those third-party services.

## Your Rights and Choices

### Language Selection
- You can select your preferred translation language through the App settings
- The selected language determines which language lyrics are translated into
- Translation functionality is a core feature and cannot be disabled

### Disclaimer
- On first launch, the App presents a disclaimer explaining data sharing practices
- You must accept the disclaimer to use the App
- Declining the disclaimer will close the application

### Stopping Data Sharing
- The only way to stop data sharing with LRCLIB.net and Google Translate is to close the App
- While the App is running and media is playing, data will be automatically shared with these services

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these services to understand how they handle your data.
